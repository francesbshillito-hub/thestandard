About

Built from inside
the industry.

The Standard was founded by Frances — a music industry consultant, artist manager and specialist trainer who has spent years working at the intersection of music, safety and advocacy.

This isn't generic corporate training. This is specialist knowledge, industry experience and personal commitment to making music a safer space for everyone in it.

Services
01

Safeguarding
Training

Specialist safeguarding training tailored for music and events environments. Covers recognition, response, escalation and documentation. Delivered online or in person.

02

Anti-Sexual
Assault

Comprehensive training covering consent, red flag behaviours, power dynamics, trauma-informed response and incident management. Built for real industry scenarios.

03

Women's
Safety

Focused on the specific risks facing women in music and events — from dancefloor to backstage. Practical frameworks, cultural accountability and zero tolerance policy development.

04

Artist
Standards

Bespoke safety standards documentation for artists, management and touring teams. Set expectations clearly and create a safer environment on tour, at festivals and in the studio.

05

Keynotes &
Conferences

Speaking engagements, panels and workshops at industry conferences including ADE, IMS and The Great Escape. Bringing the conversation to where the industry gathers.

06

Consultancy
& Advisory

Strategic safeguarding consultancy for labels, venues, festivals and booking agencies. Policy development, cultural audits and ongoing advisory support.
